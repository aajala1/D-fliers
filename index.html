D-Fliers

AI-Driven Flight Delay Prediction Model for Climate-Resilient Airspace Management

Welcome to D-Fliers!

This project researches the use of machine learning and artificial intelligence to predict weather-induced flight delays and improve climate resilience in airspace management. Using historical flight data, real-time weather reports, and air traffic information, we have developed an AI predictive model to estimate potential disruptions which cause flight delays.
