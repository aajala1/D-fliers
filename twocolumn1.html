D-Fliers is a student-led research team using artificial intelligence to address challenges in air travel reliability, particularly those driven by climate change. Our predictive models leverage historical flight data from the BTS and weather data from the Visual Crossing API to estimate potential delays before they happen. With the rise in climate-induced disruptions, we believe it's time to bridge data science, environmental awareness, and aviation planning.
