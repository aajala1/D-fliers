About the D-Fliers Project

We are an interdisciplinary team harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to mitigate the impact of weather-related flight delays and promote climate-resilient aviation systems.

To leverage AI and real-time climate data to predict and reduce flight delays, supporting smarter, safer, and more climate-resilient air travel for all stakeholders.
